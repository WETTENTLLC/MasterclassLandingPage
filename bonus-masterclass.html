Course Overview

MD Prosthetics Toe Nail Training

Duration: 2 Days

Objective: Equip participants with advanced prosthetic toenail application techniques to provide high-quality, durable solutions for aesthetic or medical needs.

  • Anatomy and Health of the Toenail: Understanding the structure and common issues of toenails.
  • Assessment of Nail Conditions: Identifying conditions that can benefit from prosthetic solutions.
  • Prosthetic Nail Technology and Materials: Overview of the latest materials and technology used in prosthetic nail applications.
  • Application Techniques: Hands-on training in applying prosthetic nails effectively.
  • Safety and Sanitation Protocols: Ensuring cleanliness and safety in procedures.
  • Client Consultation and Customization: Techniques for effective communication and tailoring services to individual client needs.
Build a 6 Figure Brand

Duration: 1 Day

Objective: Teach participants how to develop and scale a beauty brand to achieve six-figure revenues with strategic marketing and branding.

  • Brand Identity and Market Positioning: Creating a unique brand persona and positioning it in the market.
  • Targeting and Niche Marketing: Identifying and targeting specific customer segments to effectively tailor marketing efforts.
  • Specialization Strategies: Focusing on a specific area of beauty services to differentiate from competitors and enhance expert status.
  • Customer Relationship Management: Strategies for building lasting relationships with clients.
  • Pricing and Revenue Models: Setting prices and developing revenue strategies that maximize profitability.
  • Legal and Ethical Business & Branding: Understanding the legal considerations and ethical standards in business and branding.
90 Days to Launch a 6 Figure Product-Service Based Business

Duration: Online Self-paced (Access for 90 days)

Objective: Provide a step-by-step blueprint to launch and scale a product or service-based business in under 90 days.

  • Market Research and Analysis: Gathering and analyzing data to understand the market.
  • Product Development and Service Design: Creating products or services that meet customer needs.
  • Branding and Pre-launch Marketing: Building a brand and marketing it before launch.
  • Sales Funnel Creation: Developing a funnel to attract and convert customers.
  • Operational Management: Managing day-to-day operations effectively.
  • Post-launch Growth Strategies: Strategies for sustaining and growing the business after launch.
